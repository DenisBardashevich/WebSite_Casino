Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how FantasyChampions collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website or engage in fantasy sports activities.

1. Information Collection

We collect personal data when you use our services, including your name, email address, payment information, and fantasy sports preferences. This data is used to process your transactions and improve your experience with us.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us provide you with personalized recommendations for fantasy sports leagues, game strategies, and match updates. We do not sell or share your information with third parties, except where necessary to complete a transaction, such as with payment processors or delivery services.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data. While we strive to safeguard your information, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. We encourage you to review our security practices periodically.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience and track website usage. You can manage cookie settings through your browser’s preferences. Cookies help us optimize the FantasyChampions platform to enhance your gaming experience.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites that are not controlled by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external websites. Please review their privacy policies before interacting with them.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. If you have any concerns about how we handle your information, please contact us via the details below.

7. Compliance and Data Protection

At FantasyChampions, we are committed to ensuring full compliance with all applicable laws and regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We prioritize the protection of your data and privacy by implementing industry-standard security measures to safeguard your personal information.

8. Data Collection and Usage

We collect, store, and process personal information strictly in accordance with legal requirements and ethical standards. Our data-handling practices are regularly reviewed to maintain compliance with evolving regulations and industry best practices.

9. Transparency and Accountability

Transparency is at the core of our operations. We clearly outline how your data is collected, used, and stored. Our policies reflect our ongoing efforts to maintain compliance and accountability.

10. User Rights and Choices

As part of our commitment to compliance, we respect your rights to access, update, and delete your personal information. You can request data modifications or removals by contacting us at contact@FantasyChampions.com.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be updated accordingly. Please review this page periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@FantasyChampions.com.
