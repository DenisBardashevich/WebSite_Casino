The time it takes to process withdrawals varies depending on the selected method:

To manage risk and ensure platform integrity, VictoryBet has set withdrawal limits which may consist of:

  • Daily Withdrawal Limit: Users can withdraw up to $2,000 per day.

7. Security Protocols

To ensure the safety of our users, VictoryBet implements strict security measures:

  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Activate 2FA on your account for additional security.

Users may have the option to cancel a withdrawal request if it has not yet been processed. To do this, follow the steps below:

Once canceled, the funds will be returned to your account balance.
